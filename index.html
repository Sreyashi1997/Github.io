IPL T20 Data Exploration using SQL

In this project, I performed a comprehensive data exploration and analysis of T20 cricket matches from the past three years using SQL. The aim was to uncover key trends, performance metrics, and insights from the vast dataset of T20 matches.

The primary objective was to analyze the data to identify trends in player and team performances, match outcomes, and other significant cricketing metrics. The analysis also aimed to provide insights into the evolving dynamics of T20 cricket over the last three years.

LinkedIn Job Analysis Using Power BI

In this project, I conducted a comprehensive analysis of the India job market using data from LinkedIn. The goal was to uncover key trends and insights to help job seekers, employers, and analysts understand the current landscape of job opportunities.

The main objective was to analyze job postings, application trends, top fields, and the most sought-after job titles. Additionally, the project aimed to identify the best time periods for job postings and the companies receiving the highest number of applications.
